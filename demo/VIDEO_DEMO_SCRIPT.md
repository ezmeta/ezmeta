# EZ Meta Video Demo Script

## Overview
- **Duration:** 60-90 seconds
- **Format:** Screen recording only
- **Purpose:** Meta Review & Marketing

## Timeline & Script

### 00:00 - 00:10 (Hook)
**Visual:** Show EZ Meta Landing Page
**Script:** "Memperkenalkan EZ Meta, pembantu AI yang mengoptimumkan iklan Facebook & Instagram anda berdasarkan data sebenar."
**Action:** Scroll down slightly to show the main features section

### 00:10 - 00:30 (Data Flow)
**Visual:** Show Dashboard with graphs and metrics
**Script:** "EZ Meta menghubungkan akaun iklan anda dan memantau metrik kritikal seperti Spend, CTR, dan CPC dalam satu dashboard yang bersih."
**Action:** Click the "Sync" button to demonstrate data refreshing
**Note:** Pause briefly to show the updated metrics

### 00:30 - 00:50 (AI Magic)
**Visual:** Navigate to Meta Ads page showing campaigns with performance metrics
**Script:** "Apabila iklan anda tidak berprestasi, AI Creative Studio kami akan menganalisis puncanya dan menjana copywriting serta idea visual baru untuk meningkatkan ROI anda."
**Action:** Click the "🪄 Generate Creative" button on an ad with low CTR
**Note:** Wait for the modal to appear with analysis

### 00:50 - 01:10 (Multi-format)
**Visual:** Show tabs in the Creative Studio modal
**Script:** "Dapatkan segalanya dari headlines hingga ke skrip video pendek yang direka khusus untuk audiens anda."
**Action:** Click through the Copywriting, Visual Ideas, and Video Script tabs
**Note:** Pause briefly on each tab to show the content

### 01:10 - End (Call to Action)
**Visual:** Navigate to Pricing page
**Script:** "Kurangkan kos, tingkatkan jualan. Mula secara percuma dengan EZ Meta hari ini."
**Action:** Hover over the "Get Started Free" button
**Note:** End with a clean view of the pricing options

## Recording Tips
1. Ensure smooth transitions between pages
2. Keep mouse movements deliberate and minimal
3. Allow time for content to load fully before narration
4. Record at 1080p resolution minimum
5. Use a clean browser window without unnecessary tabs or bookmarks
